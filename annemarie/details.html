Slow Landscapes

2015 • The Netherlands

With myself and my camera I literally and figuratively walk into the landscape searching for that one spot, that one moment in which I coincide with the landscape, the loneliness, the peace, the space and the apparent emptiness.

And also looking for the rhythm in the landscape, the lines, the beauty of what is, the colours or the absence of colours and of course the sky’s which surprise me over and over again.

This landscape is one of my favorites, shot in the wild.

I cannot find it, don’t look any further, turn back to return to where I came from. And then, all of a sudden, it appears, the decisive moment and it drops: my eye on landscape….
